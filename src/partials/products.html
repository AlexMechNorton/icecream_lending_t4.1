  • ice cream

    Both children and adults like to eat delicious and delicate ice cream. It is perfectly refreshing in the summer heat. Eat - and you can't tear yourself away!

    STRAWBERRY-BANANA ICE CREAM Strawberries (fresh or frozen) - 150 g.; Ripe banana - 1 pc.; Natural (Greek) yogurt - 150 g.; Honey - to taste (about 1 tablespoon). Put the banana and yogurt cut into small pieces in the blender bowl. Beat everything for 30 seconds. Add strawberries and beat for a uniform consistency is obtained. Add honey to the mixture. Divide the ice cream into portions and place in the freezer.

  • ice coffee

    Summer is the time to enjoy refreshing drinks. You should not give up your favorite drink because of the heat. Drink and you can't tear yourself away!

    CHOCOLATE ICE COFFEE Water - 400 ml.; Natural ground coffee - 2 tsp.; Cocoa powder - 2 tsp.; Vanilla ice cream - 300 g.; Cream - 200 ml.; Sugar 4 - tbsp. l; Creamy waffles - 1 pc. We brew coffee. Pour the remaining water over the cocoa powder, put it on the fire and, stirring, bring it to a boil. We are cooling. We mix everything. Fill tall, thin glasses halfway with the resulting mixture, then put ice cream in each and fill almost to the brim.

  • milkshakes

    Can you offer anything better than a cool, refreshing milkshake in the summer season? Drink milkshake and you can't tear yourself away!

    Dark chocolate - 85 g.; Ground coffee - 1 tablespoon.; Milk - 700 ml.; vanilla ice cream - 80 g. Break the chocolate into small pieces (grind 10 g of chocolate on a grater for topping). Pour milk into a saucepan, bring to a boil, add chocolate and coffee and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the chocolate melts completely. Cool down. Pour chocolate-coffee milk into a blender, add ice cream and remaining milk and mix until smooth.
